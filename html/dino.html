injection molding

an injection molding project where i made dinosaurs.

the assignment

this project was part of a manufacturing course i took focused on injection molding. for this project we were tasked with designing a part model and corresponding mold, machining the mold, and injection molding the parts. we had 3 main requirements:

  • design a part based on drawings we received from students at a local elementary school (our options were a dinosaur, flower, and flying pencil)
  • the final part must be comprised of at least 2 assembled components
  • mold at least 10 fully assembled parts without defects
