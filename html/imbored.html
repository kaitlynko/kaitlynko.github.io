i've become obsessed with drawing these random amoeba things whilst bored in class and thought it would be cool to turn it into a design i could wear. and thus, "hello i'm bored" was born . . .

i knew i wanted to incorporate some graphic text in the design but wasn't sure what it should say. in the spirit of the boredom from which these amoeba doodles were born (and coincidentally, the current state of quarantine) i figured "i'm bored" was a fitting statement.

since getting one shirt or sweatshirt custom printed for myself would be way too expensive, i decided to set up a group order for my friends to join in on. it was fun having my friends share in the excitement and it reduced the cost per item; a tru win-win! HUGE thanks to all my friends who supported me in this fun little project and bought some apparel :))
